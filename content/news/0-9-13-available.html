Wetator version 0.9.13 is available.

This release is the next big milestone on our way to version 1.0. We have changed the WPath syntax. Now the '>' sign is the default separator instead of ','.
Of course you can change this by configuration to let your existing test run without change.
Also included are some bugfixes and improvements (including the usual HtmlUnit update).
Please have a careful look at the release notes.

Enjoy testing...
