Wetator Release 4.9.0 is available.

Wetator 4.9.0 is available.
Version 4.9.0 uses the HtmlUnit 4.9.0 release. Please have a careful look at the release notes.

The HtmlUnit update implies the removal of IE support; now we support Firefox, Firefox_ESR, CHROME, EDGE. Also the Applet and ActiveX support is removed.

Please make sure to update the template and the template resources.

Enjoy smart testing...
