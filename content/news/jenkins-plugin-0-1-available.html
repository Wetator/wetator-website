Jenkins Plugin 0.1 available.

After a rather long testing phase the new Wetator Jenkins Plugin is available in version 0.1.
It creates reports for your Wetator tests in Jenkins comparable to the JUnit plugin. Additionally it affects the build stability and weather depending on the test outcomes.
